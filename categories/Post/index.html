WHY

I tried to build a Homepage for the first time in 2021, and I was naturally excited at first—the significance of a personal homepage that can be maintained for a long time seems to be more than a place to store thoughts, and it is closer to a rebellion against fragmentation. But perhaps because there was no long-term scrutiny, the few blogs that were published tentatively became old after a while. They did not become a section of my thoughts as expected. So I can’t help but think: What is the purpose of establishing a homepage?

If so, just for the record, a home page doesn’t seem necessary. With the development of the Internet today, there are too many platforms that allow us to record: not to mention the fashionable Weibo, QQ, and WeChat for the time being, some niche blogging apps such as lofter and even Zhihu are extremely convenient. But they’re not what I want - I want a self; not an interaction.

This shows the necessity of a personal homepage. It must have at least the following characteristics:

  • Freedom: It is reflected in two aspects: being able to publish what I want to publish, and being able to publish in the way I want to publish.
  • Freedom in content: Not only does it mean not being troubled by sensitive words, but also that the content described can be organized in an orderly way that fits my thinking.
  • Freedom in form: more refers to the way of arrangement - the way is also a kind of content. For example, sort by keywords and sub-keywords, classify by tag, logically represent as a dendrogram or concave graph, etc.
  • Independent: Emphasize this point because the profile does away with social (I don’t assume readers exist). I want it to be organized entirely by me, to be a place of “inward reflection”. A person’s self is his most precious place.

我在2021年首次尝试建立Homepage，最初自然是激动的——一个能够被长久维护的个人主页在当今的意义似乎总不止于一个存放思想的地方，更近于一次对碎片化的反叛。但或许因为没有经过长久的推敲，尝试性发布的几篇博客在不久之后便显得陈旧。它们并没能如期待，成为我所思所想的一个剖面。于是不禁思考：建立主页的目的，是什么？

如果说，仅仅为了记录，那么主页似乎并非必须。互联网发达的今天能够让我们记录的平台实在太多：时兴的微博、QQ、微信暂且不谈，小众一些的博客app如lofter甚至于知乎也方便至极。可它们非我所需——我期望的是自我；而非互动。

一个个人主页存在的必要性也就此展现。它至少要拥有以下特点：

  • 自由：体现在两个方面：能够发表我想发表的内容、能够按照想发表的方式发表。
    • 内容上的自由：不仅仅指不受敏感词的困扰，更在于所述的内容能够按照符合我思路的，有序的方式进行组织。
    • 形式上的自由：更多指的排布方式——方式亦是一种内容。例如按关键词和次关键词排序、按tag分类、按逻辑表示成树状图或凹入图等等。
  • 独立：着重强调这一点，是因为个人主页摒弃了社交（我不假设读者的存在）。我希望它完全由我组织，成为“向内反思”之处。一个人的自我正是其最宝贵之所在。
